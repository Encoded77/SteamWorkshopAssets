[h1]Rebalance Patches[/h1]
Opinionated balance tweaks and cross-mod compatibility patches, aimed at getting a big pile of mods to play nicely together.

{{image:d10-banner-details}}

This mod touches a lot of things, and it keeps growing as more mods get patched together. If a mod misbehaves, reproduce the issue with this patch disabled before reporting it.

{{image:d20-banner-overhauls}}

{{image:d21-card-genetics-research}}

Integrated: ReSplice: Core, Gene Extractor Tiers, Gene Nodes - Genes for Sale, Gene Fabrication, Gene Ripper, and Vanilla Quests Expanded - Ancients, where a new archogen engineering capstone lets you build the archogen injector and its lab facilities yourself at archite-tier costs. Pulling them out of ancient vaults still works as the early route.

{{image:d22-card-gene-dedup}}

The gene list gets a lot shorter. Alpha Genes keeps its immunities and armor, Big and Small its body mechanics, the specialist Vanilla Races Expanded packs their specialty, Det's Xenotypes their quirks; Alpha Genes' animal summons go entirely, and VRE - Hussar's per-weapon aptitudes are replaced by four. WVC's strongest races have to be earned through its own events and morphs instead of walking in with a raid.

{{image:d31-card-xenotype-factions}}

Settled outlanders lean industrial, rough outlanders frontier, pirates predatory, the Empire aristocratic; deep-space xenotypes concentrate in Rimsenal's Spacer factions and Odyssey's Salvagers. Covers Det's Xenotypes, the Rimsenal xenotype packs, Vanilla Races Expanded, Highborn Xenotype, WVC - Xenotypes and Genes and Alpha Genes. Every reworked roster keeps at least a third of baseliners.

{{image:d23-card-scifi-renaming}}

Covers Big and Small - Races, Heaven and Hell, Genes & More, Yokai, Lamias, Slimes, More Xenotypes, WVC and Alpha Genes. Jotun become gigants, dvergr deepkin, svartalfs umbrakin, trolls regenerants; angels become ascendants and demons abyssals. Muspelheim becomes the Cinderhold Dominion, Niflheim the Permafrost Clans.

{{image:d24-card-expertise}}

Adds mechanitor expertises (Mechlord, Mechwright) and psycast expertises (Overchanneler, Quietist). Expertises from Alpha Skills, Hauts' Framework, Vanilla Fishing Expanded and Vanilla Gravship Expanded are folded in, and stats from Integrated Implants, Mechanoid Upgrades, Altered Carbon and Vanilla Psycasts Expanded ride along when those are loaded. Pawns keep any expertise they already picked.

{{image:d30-banner-patches}}

[list]
[*]RimIOT - Logistic Matrix: cheaper builds, basic components only, no power consumption.
[*]Altered Carbon: disables the redundant ranged shield belt from VAE Accessories in favour of the harder to get cuirassier belt, plus a slider for the casting relay's needlecasting range.
[*]GiTS Cyberbrains: only basic cyberbrains on the market, surgeries via EPOE-Forked brain surgery, post-basic cyberization at ultratech tier, harsher extreme mental break, tidied-up research tree.
[*]Odyssey: the long-range passenger shuttle gets 2000 chemfuel and 2000 kg cargo capacity.
[*]Alpha Genes: genes join vanilla genepacks, alphapacks/mixedpacks removed.
[*]Integrated Implants: slime-safe shoulder turrets, Alpha Mechs chip requirements for mechanitor implant research, levitators ignore water, signal boosters stack with AG command range genes.
[*]Rimsenal: armors and modular weapons gated behind the corp techs. Spacer trade caravans lose their mechanoid guards.
[*]Research prereq cleanups: VFE Pirates warcasket weapons, Impact Weaponry, Spacer Arsenal. Eltex weapons only on psycasters.
[*]Ideology: inspirations respect precepts, warlike factions can't roll pacifist vows.
[*]Vanilla & DLC: mixed modded mechanoid raid groups, creep joiners accept all human surgeries, gene complexity sliders, a hand-picked gene list for the VQEA archogen injector, vacuum resistance nerfs on modded armors.
[/list]

{{image:d40-banner-compatibility}}

Patches only apply when the mods they target are loaded, so nothing here is a hard dependency and anything you don't have installed is skipped. Every tweak has its own toggle in the mod settings (restart required), and the settings menu hides toggles whose requirements aren't met.

{{image:d45-shot-settings}}

Full details per toggle in the mod settings and in the documentation on GitHub.
